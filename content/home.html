GMO food

Nowadays, GMO food is a trendy in the food industry. Most people choose GMO since it’s cheaper than the nature food. However, have you ever consider the safety of GMO food?

According to wikipedia, GMO food is genetically modified food that has used genetic engineering method. It “does not occur naturally by matting and/or natural recombination (wikipedia, 2019).

Some well-known GMO foods including corn, cotton, canola, soy, sugar and beet. Some food industry using these foods to maximize the production of quantity products.

Some scientific data illustrates that genetic engineering differs in two separate ways. But only one is new genes with a well-known function are introduced into a crop, and it sometimes occur from the new genes come from some unrelated species.

However, some scientists have claim that consuming GMO food is safe. But as a consumer, when we buy GMO food, we have to be careful with the quality of the products.
